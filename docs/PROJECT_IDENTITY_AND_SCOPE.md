# Project Identity And Scope

Date: 2026-07-03

## Current Identity

CryptKeep is currently a profit-measurement and evidence-generation lab with a
guarded paper/shadow/live promotion path.

It is not yet proven to be a profitable trading bot.

## Why This Matters

The repo's strongest current capability is honest measurement:

- provenance-qualified paper evidence
- promotion gates
- work-log governance
- staged live controls
- operator status surfaces

The repo has not yet proven:

- archive-backed positive expectancy after costs
- real shadow cost/slippage behavior
- live execution reliability with capital
- profitability across a sustained out-of-sample window

## Near-Term Product Rule

Until expectancy is proven, prioritize work that improves:

- evidence velocity
- profitability discovery
- cost measurement
- safety
- recovery
- operator wake-up quality

Defer product polish, desktop packaging, and broad multi-exchange expansion
unless the work directly supports one of those priorities.

## Scope Boundary

Allowed near-term:

- paper evidence collection
- read-only data collection
- archive-first backtesting
- context-strategy research
- shadow would-be-fill recording
- operator alerts and recovery drills

Not allowed without separate accepted decision:

- live capital deployment
- automatic stage promotion
- unreviewed strategy-config changes
- broad product-surface expansion
- external-provider AI in an order-routing path

## Public Description

Use this wording until profitability evidence exists:

> CryptKeep is an evidence-first crypto trading research and operations system
> for collecting paper/shadow evidence, testing strategy hypotheses, and
> enforcing staged promotion controls before any live-capital decision.
